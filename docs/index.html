About

Prosure is a privately owned and managed consultancy specializing in the provision of expert Process Engineering and Flow Assurance services across the Energy Sector.

Founded in 2018, Prosure has successfully completed over 100 projects globally for a diverse range of clients, including those in oil and gas, carbon capture and storage, and hydrogen generation. Our expertise spans the entire project lifecycle, covering appraisal, front-end, detailed, and operational phases of design.

Our collective experience across a spectrum of varied projects, has allowed us to develop a thorough appreciation of the different risks and uncertainties that can affect the design process. Through this experience and clever application of industry standard software and bespoke in-house tools we aim to deliver robust, practical life of field engineering solutions tailored to your system.

Services

Prosure offer a range of Process Engineering and Flow Assurance services. In addition specialist services can be provided via our trusted partners:

  • Process engineering including equipment / facilities design and topsides process modelling;
  • Flow assurance including steady state and dynamic transient system assessment;
  • Production chemistry review and fluid modelling;
  • Safety reviews including HAZOP, HAZID and LOPA;
  • Operating philosophy and procedure development;
  • Peer assist and third party review;
  • Training courses;
  • Provider of specialist industry standard software including OLGA, PIPESIM, Multiflash, HYSYS, Symetery, CFD modelling etc;
  • Bespoke analysis / software solutions can also be created using Python and / or visual basic coding;
